Country Overview

Location

United Arab Emirates, Middle East. Located on the southeast coast of the Persian Gulf.

Population

Approximately 3.5+ million residents, making it the most populous city in the UAE.

Language

Arabic (Official). English widely spoken due to international population.

Religion

Islam (Official). Other religions freely practiced with many worship places.

Currency

UAE Dirham (AED). Pegged to US Dollar at 3.67 AED per dollar.

Government

Constitutional monarchy. Part of the federation governed by the Federal Supreme Council.
